Server Rules

  1. No cheating, dupes, or exploits. Report bugs immediately.
  2. Respect other players. No harassment, hate speech, or doxxing.
  3. Minimal griefing outside protected zones (follow local event rules).
  4. Market stalls must clearly list trades and follow item-economy rules.
  5. No pay-to-win. Store perks are cosmetic or QoL only.
  6. Follow Discord rules while in-game voice/text

Rules evolve with community feedback. Suggest changes in #suggestions.
