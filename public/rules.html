Enthusia Rules

By playing on the server or participating in the Discord, you agree to adhere to the following rules. Continued play means acceptance of current and future rule updates.

Section I: General Conduct (Chat & Voice)

  • 1.1 Respect & Toxicity: No hate speech, racism, sexism, general bigotry, or targeted harassment, including DMs. Swearing is allowed in moderation.
  • 1.2 Spamming: No excessive caps, flood, or in-game sound spam from things like note blocks or portals. No mic spam in voice chat.
  • 1.3 Staff Impersonation: Impersonating a staff member by nickname, skin, or false claims of authority is strictly prohibited.
  • 1.4 Advertising: No promoting other servers or unrelated media.
  • 1.5 Inappropriate Content: Skins, names, and builds must not contain nudity, hate symbols, offensive caricatures, slurs, sexual references, filter bypasses, sexual acts, or offensive text or imagery.
  • 1.6 IRL Topics & Politics: Politics, controversial world events, and heavy personal issues are prohibited so the server can stay an escape from real-world stress.
  • 1.7 Language: Public chat must remain in English so staff can moderate it properly.
  • 1.8 Begging: Do not ask staff or other players for free ranks, items, creative mode, or permissions.

Section II: Account Security & Integrity

  • 2.1 VPN Policy: VPNs are prohibited unless approved by an Admin or higher with a vouch.
  • 2.2 Zero Tolerance: Grooming, doxxing, blackmail, threats, or illegal content result in an immediate permanent ban.
  • 2.3 Account Sharing: You are responsible for your account. No account sharing and no headless alts.
  • 2.4 Complicity: Knowingly helping a rule breaker, such as storing dupe items, riding with a hacker, or profiting from cheating, is bannable.
  • 2.5 Harboring Ban Evaders: Knowingly inviting, gearing, or housing a player who is evading a ban is prohibited.
  • 2.6 Rule Encouragement: Encouraging others to break rules, hack, or exploit bugs is punishable.
  • 2.7 Falsifying Evidence: Forging screenshots, videos, or chat logs to frame another player or mislead staff results in an immediate ban.
  • 2.8 Unsafe Downloads: Do not share downloads or documents from unknown or questionable sources, since they may contain dangerous software.
  • 2.9 Personal Safety: Be careful with what you share, especially anything tied to private accounts or identifying info. Content from presumed underage members that exposes personal details may be removed for safety.

Section III: Gameplay & Mechanics

  • 3.1 PvP & Killing: PvP is enabled everywhere except Spawn and Market. Spawn killing and camping players is allowed.
  • 3.2 Griefing: Griefing, stealing, and raiding are allowed in the wilderness. The only protected areas are Spawn and Market.
  • 3.3 Duping: TNT duping is allowed. All other dupes, including item and rail dupes, are banned.
  • 3.4 Farms & Lag: No chunk loaders, including keeping chunks loaded while offline. Staff may remove unreasonably laggy farms.
  • 3.5 The End: Camping the End Portal frame is allowed. TNT duping or dropping falling blocks into the End Portal to trap or block entry is prohibited.
  • 3.6 Bug Abuse: Any discovered bug or exploit must be reported immediately through a ticket. Withholding or abusing bugs for personal or guild advantage can result in a ban and economy wipe.

Section IV: Player Market Rules

  • 4.1 One Stall Limit: Players are limited to one market stall each. Using alts to buy extra stalls is prohibited.
  • 4.2 Restock Limit: You may keep one single chest or barrel only for restocking shop supplies. General personal storage in stalls is not allowed.
  • 4.3 Anti-Loophole Pricing: Listing items at absurd prices to use shop slots as safe storage is strictly banned.
  • 4.4 Aesthetics & Obstructions: Keep your shop presentable. Do not leave shulker boxes placed on the floor after restocking.
  • 4.5 Market Violations: Repeated violations can lead to an immediate stall wipe and a stall ban until staff lifts it.

Section V: Reputation System

  • 5.1 False Rep: Reputation must reflect real trades or interactions. False positive or negative rep without a valid reason is prohibited.
  • 5.2 Manipulation & Mass Rep: Organizing groups to mass rep or review-bomb a player is strictly banned.
  • 5.3 Penalties: Repeated abuse of the reputation system can permanently remove your reputation privileges.

Section VI: Mods & Clients

  • 6.1 Allowed: Performance mods like Sodium and Iris, Simple Voice Chat, Replay Mod, Litematica for holograms and schematics only, and Xaero's Minimap Fair Play Edition.
  • 6.2 Prohibited: Hacked clients, X-Ray, ESP, Freecam, Baritone, auto-clickers, and Easy Place or Printer Mode at all times.
  • 6.3 Visual Unfair Advantages: Removing lava fog to locate Ancient Debris or other ores in lava lakes is prohibited.
  • 6.4 PvP Indicators: Mods or resource packs that show player health, damage indicators, hearts, or other players' armor durability are prohibited.
  • 6.5 Map Restrictions: Minimap entity and cave displays are prohibited. All world map mods that save terrain chunks are strictly prohibited, including Xaero's World Map, JourneyMap, VoxelMap, MapWriter, and Antique Atlas.

Enforcement & Tickets

  • Staff Protocol: Follow staff instructions immediately. Do not lie or provide fake evidence.
  • Ticket System: All disputes go through tickets, not DMs or public chat.
  • Punishments: Punishments range from warnings to permanent bans. Inventory rollbacks are granted only for major server-side issues with solid evidence.

Bottom Line

Trust and fairness keep the community alive.
