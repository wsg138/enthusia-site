About the Server

Enthusia SMP is a semi-anarchy survival server built around open wilderness gameplay, a protected Spawn and Market, and a player-run item economy.

Server features

Wilderness Rules

PvP, raiding, stealing, griefing, and even spawn camping are allowed outside Spawn and Market.

Protected Areas

Spawn and Market are the only protected zones. The rest of the world is fair game.

Duping & Exploits

TNT duping is allowed. Other dupes are banned, and bugs or exploits must be reported through a ticket immediately.

Player Market

Each player gets one stall plus one restock chest or barrel. Unreal pricing to dodge the storage rules is not allowed.

Mods & Fair Play

Common performance tools are fine, but hacked clients, X-Ray, Freecam, Baritone, Easy Place, world maps, and unfair PvP indicators are banned.

Account Integrity

No account sharing, no headless alts, no unapproved VPN use, and no helping cheaters, dupers, or ban evaders.

Tickets & Staff

Report bugs and handle disputes through tickets, not DMs. Follow staff instructions and do not falsify evidence.

Public chat must stay in English, and the rules can change over time. Continued play means acceptance of current and future rule updates.
