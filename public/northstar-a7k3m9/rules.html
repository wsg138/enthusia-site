Rules

Server Rules

Raiding, PvP, griefing, risky trades, guild rivalry, and base hunting are allowed in the right places. Cheating, harassment, exploits, and bad-faith behavior are not.

Section I

Conduct

  • No hate speech, slurs, racism, sexism, targeted harassment, sexual harassment, or bigotry in chat, voice, builds, names, skins, signs, books, or Discord.
  • Swearing is allowed in moderation. Do not use chat or voice to flood, spam, scream, mic spam, or intentionally disrupt everyone else.
  • No staff impersonation, false claims of authority, misleading nicknames, or pretending to speak for the server.
  • No advertising other servers or unrelated communities.
  • Keep public chat in English so staff can moderate it properly.
  • Avoid politics, controversial real-world arguments, and heavy personal topics in public spaces. The server should stay playable, not exhausting.
  • Do not beg staff or players for ranks, items, creative mode, permissions, or special treatment.

Section II

Safety & Accounts

  • Grooming, doxxing, blackmail, credible threats, illegal content, or attempts to expose private information result in severe punishment, including permanent bans.
  • You are responsible for your account. Account sharing, headless alts, and ban evasion are not allowed.
  • VPNs require approval from an Admin or higher. Unapproved VPN use may be blocked or punished.
  • Do not knowingly help cheaters, dupers, exploiters, or ban evaders by storing items, hiding them, gearing them, inviting them, or profiting from them.
  • Do not fake screenshots, videos, logs, or reports. False evidence damages trust and will be treated seriously.
  • Do not share suspicious downloads, documents, executables, or links that could compromise another player.

Section III

Survival Gameplay

  • Spawn and Market are protected. Outside protected areas, raiding, stealing, griefing, base hunting, and PvP are part of the server.
  • No land claims exist. A build being impressive, hidden, active, or new does not make it protected.
  • The world is permanent and is not planned to reset, but staff may remove severe lag machines, broken contraptions, or exploit damage when needed.
  • TNT duping is allowed. Item dupes, rail dupes, economy dupes, and other duplication exploits are banned unless staff explicitly says otherwise.
  • Chunk loaders and offline chunk-loading setups are not allowed.
  • Report bugs or exploits through tickets. Withholding or abusing them for personal, group, or economy advantage can lead to bans, wipes, or rollbacks.

Section IV

PvP & Warzone

  • PvP is allowed everywhere except protected Spawn and protected Market.
  • Outside the Warzone, vanilla PvP tools are allowed, including crystals, anchors, TNT, pearls, and normal combat.
  • The Main Warzone is for general PvP and does not allow building or breaking.
  • Cobweb Box allows specific utility placements such as cobwebs, water, buttons, and pressure plates. Arena blocks are not free to break.
  • Warpit allows building, breaking, carts, explosives, traps, digging, and hole fighting, but not respawn anchors, end crystals, or full crystal PvP mechanics.
  • Warzone and event rules may be enforced differently from wilderness rules because those areas are designed systems, not normal terrain.

Section V

Market & Economy

  • Raw gold is the official currency. Smelted gold is not the same currency item.
  • Use Market stalls for real shops and trades, not protected storage.
  • Do not use alts, loopholes, fake pricing, or inactive listings to bypass stall limits or store items safely.
  • Keep stalls readable and presentable. Do not leave restock clutter, blocked paths, or intentionally confusing shop setups.
  • Scams, price mistakes, and bad deals may be part of player trade, but exploits, fake evidence, and rule bypasses are not.
  • Repeated Market abuse can lead to stall wipes, stall bans, or economy action.

Section VI

Guilds

  • Guilds do not create protected land, protected bases, or staff-backed territory.
  • Do not use guild systems to bypass normal survival, Market, or economy rules.
  • Guild rivalry, KOTH competition, raids, betrayal, and conflict are allowed within the rest of the rules.
  • Do not use guild systems to launder exploited items, hide ban evaders, or bypass Market restrictions.

Section VII

Events & Duels

  • Server events use separate inventories. SMP items do not go into events, and event items do not return to SMP survival.
  • Do not exploit event maps, queues, teleporting, kits, scoring, or inventory separation.
  • Duels use player-owned gear unless the duel settings say otherwise. Read the settings before accepting.
  • Duel wagers use in-game money or gold. Staff will not rescue players from wagers they knowingly accepted unless a bug or rule break is involved.
  • Spectators near duel areas are not automatically protected. Watching a fight can still put you in danger.

Section VIII

Mods & Clients

  • Allowed examples include performance mods, shaders, Simple Voice Chat, Replay Mod, Litematica for holograms and schematics only, and fair-play minimaps without unfair displays.
  • Hacked clients, X-Ray, ESP, Freecam, Baritone, auto-clickers, Easy Place, Printer Mode, and automated combat or movement tools are banned.
  • Do not use resource packs or mods that remove lava fog to find Ancient Debris or other ores.
  • Do not use health indicators, damage indicators, armor durability indicators for other players, cave/entity radar, or world map mods that save terrain chunks.
  • If a mod gives information or actions a vanilla player could not reasonably have, ask staff before using it.

Section IX

Tickets & Enforcement

  • Use tickets for reports, bug issues, staff help, and disputes. Do not pressure staff through public chat or DMs.
  • Follow staff instructions during investigations. Lying, stalling, hiding evidence, or moving items during a ticket can make the punishment worse.
  • Punishments may include warnings, mutes, kicks, bans, inventory wipes, economy wipes, stall removal, or guild action depending on severity.
  • Staff may update rules as the server changes. Continued play means you accept the current rules.

Bottom line

Bottom Line

Enthusia is supposed to be dangerous, competitive, and player-driven. Do not confuse semi-anarchy with permission to cheat, harass, exploit, or make the community worse to be around.
